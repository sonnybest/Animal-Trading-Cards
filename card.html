Snail

Sea snails (who live in saltwater) and freshwater snails typically use gills to breathe. Some freshwater snails have both gills and a lung.

  • Scientific Name: Gastropoda
  • Number of Species: 43,000 species
  • Number of Teeth: 14,000
  • Average lifespan: 15 to 20 years

Snails belong to a group of mollusks known as gastropods.It has a soft, unsegmented body that is long, moist and slimy. The body is normally protected by a hard shell. The snail's mouth is in the middle of its head and below the lower set of tentacles. The visceral hump that contains most of the snail's vital organs is actually located inside the snail's shell.If the snail is disturbed, it can withdraw completely into its shell.
